Features

3 of the features of VidLearn lerning platform:

User Profiles

Users have personalized profiles where they can showcase their courses, track their progress, and interact with the community.

Responsive Design

The platform is designed to be responsive, ensuring that users can access and interact with content seamlessly on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Instructor Tools

Instructors have access to tools for creating and managing courses, monitoring student progress, and analyzing course performance.

About VidLearn

VidLearn is more than just a project; it's the realization of a dream to revolutionize online learning. Inspired by a passion for education and technology, I embarked on this journey to create a platform that empowers learners and instructors alike.

The project's timeline has been filled with countless hours of dedication and collaboration. I am proud to present VidLearn as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
